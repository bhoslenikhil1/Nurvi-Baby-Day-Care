🛡️ Safety & Security Rules

Your child's safety is non-negotiable.

We follow strict safety guidelines so every parent can leave in the morning with a calm mind.

  • 🚫

    No outsider allowed for pick-up/drop

    Only parents or pre-approved family members can pick up or drop a child. No exceptions.

  • 📵

    No outside visitors during care hours

    The home is closed to outside people while children are present — their safety comes first.

  • 🧼

    Hygiene & cleanliness maintained

    Regular handwashing, clean play areas, and fresh meals — we take hygiene very seriously.

  • 📞

    Immediate parent communication

    If a child feels unwell or upset, parents are contacted right away — no delays, no surprises.

  • 💛

    Gentle discipline — no harsh behaviour

    We handle children with patience, kindness and love. No shouting, no fear-based correction.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Questions parents always ask

Honest answers — because trust matters.

Is this a daycare centre or a home-based service? ⌄
This is completely home-based care — not a commercial daycare centre. Your child comes to our actual home in Aadharwadi, Kalyan West, and spends the day in a real family environment. It is more like staying at a relative's home than going to a daycare.
Who takes care of the children? ⌄
The children are cared for by grandparents — patient, experienced, and full of love. This is something very few childcare options can offer. They treat every child with the same warmth and attention they would give their own grandchild.
How many children are handled at a time? ⌄
We intentionally keep the number of children very small so that each child receives personal attention and care. This is one of our key promises — your child will never be lost in a crowd here.
What safety measures are followed? ⌄
Only pre-approved parents or family members can pick up or drop a child — no outsiders are allowed. No outside visitors enter the home during care hours. Food is freshly cooked at home. We follow hygiene routines and keep the space clean and child-safe at all times.
What if my child cries or feels uncomfortable? ⌄
This is completely normal, especially in the first few days. We give each child time to settle in with warmth, patience and gentle reassurance. We never force a child or leave them to cry alone. If needed, we will call you right away. Most children settle in within a week and start looking forward to coming here.
Can parents visit before admission? ⌄
Absolutely, yes — we encourage it! We would love for you to visit, meet us, see the home, and ask all your questions before making a decision. Call or WhatsApp us to schedule a visit. We are always happy to meet you and your child.
How are emergencies handled? ⌄
Parents are called immediately. We do not wait or delay. If a child is unwell, injured or upset, we inform the parents right away and take appropriate first-response steps. We keep emergency contact details for all enrolled children always accessible.
